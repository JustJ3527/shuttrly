Media Guidelines

Image Requirements

  • Format: JPG, PNG, GIF
  • Size: Maximum 5MB
  • Dimensions: Recommended 450x450 pixels
  • Quality: High quality, clear images work best

Content Guidelines

  • Use appropriate, non-offensive content
  • Avoid copyrighted material
  • Keep it professional and respectful
  • Your face or a clear representation is recommended
